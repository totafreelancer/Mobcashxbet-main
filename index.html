Who is an agent

Agent is an intermediary who makes money by helping players deposit and withdraw money from their accounts. The modern network marketing model allows an agent to earn money on the commission that 1xBet pays them. Using a smartphone and a special mobile app MobCash an agent can open his own mobile cash register and receive a solid income by making money transfers.
